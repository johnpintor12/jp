Military & First Responder Discount Inquiry

Guiding Customers on Discount Availability and Application

Context

Customers, including active military members, veterans, and first responders, are inquiring about the availability of a military discount, how to apply it, and any conditions or restrictions associated with it. Your role is to thank them for their service, clearly explain the discount policy, guide them on how to receive it using GovX ID, and clarify its applicability, especially concerning subscriptions and other offers.

▼

Step 1: Acknowledge the customer's inquiry and thank them for their service

Response:

  • Start with a polite and appreciative greeting: "Hi [Customer's Name],"
  • Thank them for their service: "Thank you for reaching out and asking about our discounts. And thank you for your service!"
▼

Step 2: Confirm the availability and terms of the discount

Response:

"Yes, we proudly offer a 10% discount for all military members, veterans, and first responders."

"We use GovX ID for verification. You should see the option for this in the cart window towards the bottom when you are placing an order. You can click there and verify your status in the window that pops up to receive the discount."

▼

Step 3: Explain the conditions and limitations of the discount

Key Conditions:

  • General Rule - No Combining with Other Offers: "A couple of quick notes: The discount generally can’t be combined with other offers, including the ongoing savings from subscriptions."
  • Exception for First Subscription Payment: "However, we do make an exception – you can apply the GovX verified discount to your very first subscription payment."
  • Application During Checkout: "It's important to note that the verification and discount application need to be completed during the checkout process before the order is placed."
  • Cannot be Applied Retroactively to Existing Subscriptions: "Unfortunately, once a subscription order is confirmed, the system doesn't allow us to go back and apply that specific GovX-verified discount retroactively, even to the first payment if it wasn't applied at checkout."
  • Use on Future One-Time Orders: "You can certainly use the GovX ID verification process to receive the discount on future one-time (non-subscription) orders you place through our website. Just be sure to complete the verification in the cart before finalizing your payment next time you place a one-time order."
▼

Step 4: Address specific scenarios

If a customer asks if they can use the military discount and another discount code together:

Response:

"Thank you so much for your service! We’re proud to offer a 10% discount for all military members and first responders. A couple of quick notes: The discount can’t be combined with other offers, including other discount codes or subscriptions. However, we do make an exception—you can apply it to your first subscription payment."

If a customer placed an order and forgot to apply the GovX ID discount (especially a subscription):

Response:

"Thank you for your order (#[Order Number if provided]) and for reaching out about the discount! Also, thank you for your service!"

"Regarding applying it to your recent subscription order: the verification and discount application need to be completed during the checkout process before the order is placed. Unfortunately, once a subscription order is confirmed, the system doesn't allow us to go back and apply that specific GovX-verified discount retroactively, even to the first payment. We apologize for any confusion if the placement of the GovX ID button seemed unclear during checkout!"

"Generally, this discount also cannot be combined with the ongoing savings already included in subscription orders (beyond the first payment exception if applied at checkout). However, you can certainly use the GovX ID verification process to receive the discount on future one-time (non-subscription) orders you place through our website."

▼

Step 5: Offer further assistance and conclude

Concluding Interaction:

  • End with: "Let us know if you have any further questions, we’re happy to help!"
  • Thank them again: "Thank you again for your service and for choosing our products."
▼

Step 6: Document and tag the interaction (Internal)

Internal Actions:

  • Tag the conversation with relevant keywords (e.g., "Military Discount," "First Responder Discount," "GovX ID," "Discount Inquiry," "Subscription Discount"). TAG
  • Note key details: customer's service status (if confirmed), specific question, information provided, and resolution.
