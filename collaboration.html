Business Proposal & Wholesale Inquiry Workflow

Guiding External Parties on Collaboration and Wholesale Processes

Context

External individuals, companies, or organizations are reaching out with business proposals, partnership ideas, wholesale inquiries, or requests for business collaborations (e.g., displaying products in stores, bulk purchases). Your role as a CX Support Specialist is to acknowledge their interest and direct them to the appropriate channel, particularly for wholesale inquiries, using the established process.

▼

Step 1: Acknowledge the inquiry and express appreciation

Response:

  • Start with a professional and courteous greeting: "Hi [Contact Person's Name/Company Name], thank you for reaching out to H2Tab and for your interest in exploring a potential [business proposal/collaboration/wholesale opportunity] with us."
  • Acknowledge the nature of their inquiry: "I understand you're looking to discuss a [business proposal/collaboration idea/wholesale account/bulk purchase/stocking our products]."
▼

Step 2: Explain the process for submitting B2B/Wholesale inquiries

Response for Wholesale/B2B:

"Thank you for your interest in bringing H2Tab to your community! We’re excited to learn more about your business. Our molecular hydrogen tablets are designed to support energy, focus, and overall wellness—offering a convenient, cost-effective, and science-backed solution your customers can trust."

Provide the specific link for the Wholesale Program application:
"To get started with H2Tab's Wholesale Program, just fill out this quick form (it only takes a minute): https://h2tabwholesale1.paperform.co/"

Explain the next steps after form submission:
"Once submitted, we’ll send you a follow-up email with next steps to move forward. Our team typically reviews applications within 3–7 business days and will reach out with wholesale access and product details."

If the customer mentions issues with the first form or needs the second step:
"So sorry for the inconvenience, if you are looking for the next steps in our wholesale process, please use this link: https://h2tabwholesale2.paperform.co/"

If a customer claims to already have a wholesale account (and you need to clarify):
"Thank you for your message. We completely understand the confusion and have double-checked with our internal team to be sure. At this time, we do not have active wholesale accounts or wholesale pricing available through that previous understanding—we’re not offering wholesale just yet through general accounts, though it’s something we plan to roll out more broadly in the near future. The correct process for all new wholesale inquiries is through the application form I mentioned. It sounds like there may have been some miscommunication, and we’d love to better understand what happened. Would you mind letting us know who you spoke with previously about setting up a wholesale account? That’ll help us make sure we’re all aligned moving forward."

▼

Step 3: For other types of business proposals or collaborations (non-wholesale)

If the inquiry is for a non-wholesale business proposal/collaboration:

"Thank you for sharing the details of your [proposal/collaboration idea]. For these types of initiatives that are not related to our wholesale program, I will ensure your email and the information you've provided are forwarded directly to our [Relevant Department, e.g., Business Development Team or Partnerships Department at partnerships@yourcompany.com or info@drinkh2tab.com - adjust as per your actual internal routing and if you name the dept/email to the customer]. They handle all such proposals and will review your submission. If there's a potential fit or if they require more information, they will reach out to you directly."

▼

Step 4: Clarify the customer support team's role

Managing Expectations:

"Our customer support team is primarily focused on assisting customers with product inquiries, order support, and general questions about our services. Therefore, we don't directly handle the review or negotiation of business proposals or collaborations beyond guiding you to the correct application process or forwarding your initial inquiry."

"Using the wholesale application form for those inquiries, or having your proposal forwarded as I've mentioned, will ensure it's seen by the appropriate team."

▼

Step 5: Conclude the interaction professionally

Concluding Interaction:

  • Thank them again: "Thank you once again for considering H2Tab. We appreciate you taking the time to share your interest/proposal with us."
  • Set expectations for follow-up: "Our team will review applications/proposals and typically reach out within [mention timeframe if known, e.g., 3-7 business days for wholesale applications, or a more general timeframe for other proposals] if there is an alignment with our current strategic objectives or if further information is needed."
  • Offer to help with CX-related questions: "If you have any questions related to our products or customer services, please feel free to ask."
▼

Step 6: Document and tag the interaction (Internal)

Internal Actions:

Tag the conversation with relevant keywords (e.g., "Business Proposal," "Wholesale Inquiry," "Partnership," "B2B," "Collaboration," etc.). TAG

Note key details: contact person/company, nature of proposal, links provided, department forwarded to (if applicable), and any immediate impressions or red flags.
