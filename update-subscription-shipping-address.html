Update Subscription Shipping Address

Order Management

Context

A customer wants to change the shipping address for their subscription's future orders. Your job is to guide them to the customer portal where they can manage this themselves.

Instructions

1 Acknowledge and Guide to Self-Service

Response to Customer:

"Hi [Customer Name], I can certainly help with that. You can update the shipping address for your subscription at any time through your secure customer portal."

2 Provide Portal Link and Instructions

Continue Response:

"Once you log in, just navigate to the 'Subscriptions' section and you'll find the option to edit your shipping details for all upcoming orders. Here is the direct link to your account portal: [Link to Customer Portal Login]."

3 Offer Further Assistance and Conclude

Conclude Response:

"If you have any trouble finding it, please let me know!"
