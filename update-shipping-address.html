Update Shipping Address

Category: Order Management

Context

A customer wants to change the shipping address for an order they have already placed. Your job is to determine if the address change is possible based on the order's fulfillment status and then either perform the update or clearly explain why it cannot be done.

▼

Step 1: Identify Order and Check Fulfillment Status

First, identify the relevant order using the customer's email or order number. Once the order is located, check its fulfillment status.

Decision Logic:

  • If Fulfillment Status is "Unfulfilled" AND the order does NOT have the "in progress" tag: The address can be changed. PROCEED to Step 2, Path A.
  • If Fulfillment Status is "Fulfilled," "Shipped," or has the "in progress" tag: The address cannot be changed by the support team. PROCEED to Step 2, Path B.
▼

Step 2: Communicate with Customer and Perform Action

Path A: Address Change is Possible

  1. Action: Ask the customer for the complete new shipping address.
  2. Once the new address is received, update it in the order details.
  3. Respond to Customer: "Hi [Customer Name], I was able to update the shipping address for your order #[Order Number]. It will now be sent to: [Paste New Address Here]. You'll receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking once it's on its way. Please let me know if there's anything else I can help with!"
  4. Internal Action: Mark ticket as 'Solved'.

Path B: Address Change is Not Possible

  1. Respond to Customer: "Hi [Customer Name], I've checked on your order #[Order Number], and it has already been processed by our fulfillment team and is being prepared for shipment. Because it's already in the shipping process, I'm unable to change the address in our system. Your best option is to contact the carrier directly with your tracking number once you receive it. They can sometimes reroute a package. I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause."
  2. Internal Action: Mark ticket as 'Solved'.
