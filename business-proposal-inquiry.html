Business Proposal Inquiry

Category: Business Inquiries

Context

A customer, influencer, or other party is reaching out with a business proposal, such as a partnership, affiliate request, or wholesale inquiry. Your job is to acknowledge their message professionally and route it to the correct internal team for review, as you are not equipped to handle these discussions directly.

Instructions

Step 1: Acknowledge and Route the Customer

Respond to the customer with a polite, standardized message that sets expectations.

Response to Customer:

"Thank you so much for your interest in partnering with H2Tab! We're always excited to explore new opportunities. I've forwarded your message to our partnerships team, who will review your proposal and get back to you directly if it's a good fit. We appreciate you reaching out!"

Step 2: Escalate Internally to Partnerships Team

After responding to the customer, create an internal escalation to the appropriate team.

  • Action: Assign the ticket to the "Partnerships" or "Business Development" team/agent.
  • Internal Note: Add a note to the ticket: "Business proposal received from [Customer Name/Email]. Forwarded for review as per protocol. Elisha has sent the initial acknowledgment."
  • Ticket Status: Leave ticket status as 'Open' for the internal team to handle.
