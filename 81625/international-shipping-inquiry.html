International Shipping Inquiry

Category: Shipping & Delivery

Context

A customer is asking if H2Tab ships to their country. Your job is to determine if their country is on the list of supported shipping destinations and provide a clear, helpful response, either confirming that you ship there or politely explaining that you do not and offering to record their interest.

â–¼

Instructions

Step 1: Identify the Customer's Country

If the customer has not already mentioned their country, ask them:

"I can definitely check that for you! What country are you looking to ship to?"

Step 2: Check Country Against Supported List and Respond

Compare the customer's country to the current list of supported international shipping destinations: Canada, UK, Australia, New Zealand, Germany, France, Spain, Italy, Netherlands, Japan.

Path A: If Country IS on the list

"Great news! Yes, we do ship to [Customer's Country]. You can proceed with your order directly on our website, and the shipping options and costs will be calculated for you at checkout."

Path B: If Country is NOT on the list

"Thanks for checking! At the moment, we do not currently offer shipping to [Customer's Country]. We are actively working on expanding our international shipping capabilities, and I have added your interest for [Customer's Country] to our expansion list. We truly appreciate you reaching out and hope to be able to serve you in the future!"

Step 3: Conclude the Conversation

End the interaction with a helpful closing:

"Let me know if there is anything else I can help you with!"
