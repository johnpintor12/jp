Subscription Management - General Inquiry

Category: Order Management

Context

A customer asks a general question about managing their subscription, such as how to view it, make changes, or see upcoming orders. Your job is to empower the customer to manage their own subscription by directing them to the customer portal and explaining what they can do there.

▼

Step 1: Acknowledge and Inform

Response to Customer:

"Hi [Customer Name], thanks for reaching out! You can easily manage all aspects of your subscription directly through your customer account portal."

▼

Step 2: Provide Self-Service Link and Explain Capabilities

Continue Response:

"In the portal, you can update your shipping address, change your payment method, adjust your order frequency, skip a shipment, or cancel your subscription at any time. You can log in to your account right here: [Link to Customer Portal Login]."

▼

Step 3: Offer Further Assistance and Conclude

Conclude Response:

"If you have any trouble logging in or can't find what you're looking for, please let me know and I'd be happy to help further!"
