Update Subscription Payment Method

Category: Order Management

Context

A customer wants to update the credit card or payment information for their active subscription. Your job is to guide them to the specific section of the customer portal where they can securely make this change themselves.

▼

Step 1: Acknowledge and Guide

Response to Customer:

"Hi [Customer Name], I can certainly help with that. You can update your payment method for your subscription at any time through your secure customer portal."

▼

Step 2: Provide Direct Link and Instructions

Continue Response:

"Once you log in, just navigate to the 'Payment Methods' section to add your new card details. Here is the direct link to your account portal: [Link to Customer Portal Login]."

▼

Step 3: Offer Further Assistance and Conclude

Conclude Response:

"If you run into any issues, just let me know!"
