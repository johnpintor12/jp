International Shipping Inquiry Workflow

Guiding Customers on International & Military Shipping Options

Context

Customers are inquiring about shipping products internationally or to military/diplomatic addresses. Your role is to clearly explain that our direct website store does not offer international shipping. Instead, you will guide customers to our Amazon store for most international orders, including all shipments to APO/FPO/DPO addresses. For customers in the UK, you will also inform them about our local partner, leaf.com, in addition to Amazon's general international availability. You'll also need to direct customers to the appropriate support channels for issues based on where they placed their order.

▼

Step 1: Understand the specific nature of the international shipping inquiry

Initial Question to Customer:

"Could you please provide details about your international shipping inquiry? For example, are you asking about shipping to a specific country, military address, or do you have questions about an existing international order? If it's an existing order, please provide the order number."

▼

Step 2: Process the inquiry based on the customer's response

IF the inquiry is about general international shipping or a specific country (including the UK):

Response:

"Our website store currently doesn't support direct international shipping. However, we recommend checking our product listings on Amazon for international shipping options. Amazon provides fulfillment to many countries and calculates shipping costs, delivery estimates, and any applicable import fees at checkout."

IF the customer mentions the UK:

Add: "For UK customers, in addition to Amazon, you can also purchase from our local UK partner, leaf.com."

IF the inquiry is about shipping to APO/FPO/DPO addresses:

Response:

"For all shipments to APO, FPO, and DPO addresses, please place your order through our Amazon store. When ordering, ensure you format the address correctly according to military guidelines. Amazon's checkout process will guide you through this. Delivery times and tracking for these orders are subject to Amazon's policies and military postal service operations."

IF the inquiry is about an existing order:

Action:

Use the 'Get Order Info' action to retrieve order details.

IF the order was placed through Amazon:

Direct the customer: "Since your order was placed through Amazon, they manage all aspects of fulfillment, shipping, and customer service. Please contact Amazon Customer Service directly through your Amazon account for assistance with tracking, delivery, returns, or refunds."

IF the order was placed through leaf.com:

Advise: "For orders placed with leaf.com, please reach out to their customer service directly for assistance with your order."

IF the customer cannot find an option for their country via Amazon or leaf.com:

Response:

"Currently, Amazon and our UK partner leaf.com are our established channels for international and UK sales respectively. If your specific country isn't serviceable through these channels, unfortunately, we may not have a shipping option to your location at this time."

▼

Step 3: Document the interaction and tag the ticket

Internal Actions:

Tag the conversation based on the inquiry type:

  • "International Shipping Inquiry - General" TAG
  • "International Shipping Inquiry - UK" TAG
  • "International Shipping Inquiry - APO/FPO/DPO" TAG
  • "Amazon Referral - International" TAG
  • "UK Partner Referral - leaf.com" TAG
  • "Existing Order Inquiry - International" TAG

Document key details:

  • Customer's specific question
  • Destination country/address type
  • Purchasing channel advised (Amazon or leaf.com)
  • Information provided
  • Any follow-ups planned
