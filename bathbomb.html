Hydrogen Bath Bomb Inquiry Workflow

Visualized Logic for Handling Customer Questions about Upcoming Launch

Context

Customers are reaching out to inquire about the availability in the website, release date, or details of the upcoming hydrogen bath bombs. Your role is to generate excitement by announcing the upcoming launch, provide the direct link for customers to sign up for early access and updates, and manage expectations regarding the exact release date or full ingredient list by explaining that those aren't available just yet as the product is pre-launch.

▼

Step 1: Acknowledge the customer's interest and share the exciting news

Response:

  • Begin by thanking the customer for their interest in the new hydrogen bath bomb.
  • Deliver the positive news using this revised statement:
    "Thank you for reaching out. Our team is working hard behind the scenes to bring you our latest innovations. And we have great news - our hydrogen bath bombs are launching soon!"
▼

Step 2: Direct the customer to sign up for early access and updates

Action & Response:

  • Encourage them to be among the first to know and shop by guiding them to the sign-up page:
    "For updates and to be one of the first to shop, you can sign up for early access here: https://drinkh2tab.com/pages/hydrogen-bath-bomb-notification"
▼

Step 3: Address inquiries about a specific release date or ingredients

If customer asks for a specific release date (or proactively set expectations):

  • "I understand you're eager for a specific timeframe, and we'd love to give you one!"
  • "However, for new developments like the hydrogen bath bombs, while the team is working hard to bring them out as soon as possible, we don't have an exact anticipated release date that we can announce just yet. Apologies we can't be more specific right now!"
  • Reinforce sign-up value: "The best way to know right away when a launch date is set is definitely by signing up for notifications via the link I shared."

If the customer asks about the ingredients:

  • "That's a great question! Since the hydrogen bath bomb is not yet launched, we aren't sharing the full list of specific ingredients publicly at this moment."
  • "Detailed product information, including the complete ingredient list, will be made available closer to or at the official launch. By signing up on the early access page I mentioned (https://drinkh2tab.com/pages/hydrogen-bath-bomb-notification), you'll be among the first to receive all these details as soon as they are released."
▼

Step 4: Offer further assistance and conclude positively

Concluding Interaction:

  • Close on a positive and appreciative note:
    "Thanks so much for your patience and excitement for this new launch!"
  • Offer to answer any other questions they might have:
    "Let me know if you have any other questions!"
